# Test 1
1g in 2 marble chips was measured using a very accurate scale. 10ml of 40% acetic acid was then placed in a beaker. The beaker was placed on the scale, and marble chips were taken off before the scale was tared. The marble chips were then placed in. This was to see how much mass would be lost in an unspecified time and if all mass would be lost. After a bit, I put it on a heated copper plate to speed up the process, and after a while, the marble chips were mostly dissolved, and it was 3g lighter.

Total time contributed: 0.25 hours
